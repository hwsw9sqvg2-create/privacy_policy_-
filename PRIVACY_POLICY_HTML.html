1. Introduction

NUM CALENDAR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

NUM CALENDAR is designed to work completely offline. We collect minimal information that is stored locally on your device:

2.1 User Preferences (Stored Locally Only)

  • Birth date (day, month, year) - used only for numerology calculations
  • Selected target month and year - for calendar display
  • Language preference (English, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish)

2.2 No Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Activity tracking data
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Any data from other apps

3. How We Use Your Information

All data you enter is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This information is used solely to:

  • Calculate numerology readings based on your birth date
  • Display the calendar for your selected month and year
  • Remember your language preference

We do NOT:

  • Transmit any data over the internet
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising
  • Sell data to anyone
  • Access data from other apps

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iOS device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by iOS security features. You can delete the app at any time to remove all stored data.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. All functionality is self-contained within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Modify your data (through the app settings)

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control over it.
